How to make homemade pizza 4 cheese

Four cheese pizza is a classic pizza recipe that features a variety of delicious cheeses. The pizza crust is made with flour, yeast, salt, and water and is rolled out into a thin round shape. The toppings include a mixture of four different types of cheeses, such as mozzarella, Parmesan, Gouda, and blue cheese. To make the pizza, the crust is first baked in a hot oven until crispy. Then, the mixture of cheeses is spread evenly on top of the crust, making sure to cover the entire pizza. Finally, the pizza is topped with a drizzle of olive oil and sometimes with herbs like rosemary or thyme. Four cheese pizza is a popular and flavorful option that is perfect for cheese lovers. The combination of different types of cheese creates a unique and delicious taste in every bite. It can be served as a main dish for a family dinner or as an appetizer for a party.

prep time

24 h.

cook time

30 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Pizza Dough and toppings

instructions

step 1

Make the pizza dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine 3 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of salt, 1 teaspoon of sugar, 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil, and 1 teaspoon of active dry yeast. Gradually add 1 and 1/4 cups of warm water while stirring until the dough comes together. Knead the dough for 10-15 minutes until smooth and elastic. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it rest at room temperature for 10 minutes before refrigerating for 24 hours.

step 2

Prepare the tomato sauce: In a saucepan, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add 3 minced garlic cloves and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Pour in a 28-ounce can of crushed San Marzano tomatoes and 1 teaspoon of salt. Bring the sauce to a simmer and cook for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens.

step 3

Roll out the pizza dough: Place the rolled-out dough on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Add 4 ounces of shredded mozzarella cheese, 4 ounces of shredded fontina cheese, 4 ounces of shredded gouda cheese, and 4 ounces of crumbled blue cheese on top of the sauce. Sprinkle with a pinch of salt and a drizzle of olive oil.

step 4

Bake the pizza: Transfer the pizza to the preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Top with a sprinkle of fresh thyme leaves before slicing and serving. Enjoy your homemade 4 cheese pizza!

ingredients

Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought) | Flour 1kg | 600 ml of water | 5 gr of fresh yeast | 25 gr salt

Tomato sauce

Fresh mozzarella cheese | Parmesan cheese | Gorgonzola cheese | Goat cheese

Fresh basil leaves | Garlic cloves | Thime

Extra-virgin olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

Note that you can customize the ingredients based on your preferences or dietary restrictions.

tools

Stand mixer or large mixing bowl: to mix the pizza dough ingredients

Rolling pin or pizza dough roller: to flatten and shape the pizza dough

Pizza stone or baking sheet: to bake the pizza on

Pizza peel or flat, sturdy cutting board: to transfer the pizza onto the pizza stone or baking sheet

Oven mitts or kitchen towels: to handle the hot pizza stone or baking

Pizza cutter or sharp knife: to slice the pizza after it's baked

Note that some of these tools may be substituted with other kitchen tools you already have at home.
