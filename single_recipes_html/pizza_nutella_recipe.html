How to make homemade pizza with Nutella

Nutella pizza is a sweet and indulgent dessert pizza that is popular in Italy and around the world. The pizza dough is typically made with flour, yeast, sugar, and water, and is rolled out into a thin round shape. Nutella, a creamy chocolate and hazelnut spread, is then generously spread over the pizza dough. The pizza is baked in a hot oven until the crust is crispy and the Nutella is melted and gooey. Some variations of Nutella pizza may also include sliced bananas, strawberries, or other fruits as toppings, as well as a dusting of powdered sugar or a drizzle of chocolate syrup. It's a decadent treat that is sure to satisfy any sweet tooth and is perfect for sharing with friends and family.

prep time

24 h.

cook time

30 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Pizza Dough and toppings

instructions

step 1

Make the pizza dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine 3 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of salt, 1 teaspoon of sugar, 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil, and 1 teaspoon of active dry yeast. Gradually add 1 and 1/4 cups of warm water while stirring until the dough comes together. Knead the dough for 10-15 minutes until smooth and elastic. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it rest at room temperature for 10 minutes before refrigerating for 24 hours.

step 2

Prepare the Nutella spread: In a microwave-safe bowl, add 1 cup of Nutella and microwave for 20-30 seconds until it becomes smooth and spreadable.

step 3

Roll out the pizza dough: Place the rolled-out dough on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Spread wather and a bit of olive oil evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle with a pinch of salt and a drizzle of olive oil.

step 4

Bake the pizza: Preheat your oven to 500Â°F. Transfer the pizza to the preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is crispy. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

step 5

Add the toppings: Top with the melted Nutella and fresh fruit of your choice, such as strawberries or bananas, before slicing and serving. Enjoy your homemade Nutella pizza!

ingredients

Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought) | Flour 1kg | 600 ml of water | 5 gr of fresh yeast | 25 gr salt

Nutella (or any chocolate-hazelnut spread)

Powdered sugar

Fresh fruit (such as sliced strawberries, bananas, or raspberries)

Chopped nuts (such as hazelnuts, almonds, or walnuts)

Whipped cream (optional)

Note that you can customize the ingredients based on your preferences or dietary restrictions.

tools

Stand mixer or large mixing bowl: to mix the pizza dough ingredients

Rolling pin or pizza dough roller: to flatten and shape the pizza dough

Pizza stone or baking sheet: to bake the pizza on

Pizza peel or flat, sturdy cutting board: to transfer the pizza onto the pizza stone or baking sheet

Oven mitts or kitchen towels: to handle the hot pizza stone or baking

Pizza cutter or sharp knife: to slice the pizza after it's baked

Note that some of these tools may be substituted with other kitchen tools you already have at home.
