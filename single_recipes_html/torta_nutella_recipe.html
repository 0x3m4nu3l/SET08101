How to make Spaghetti alle Vongole

Torta alla Nutella is a classic Italian dessert made with Nutella, a popular hazelnut chocolate spread. The cake is typically made with a combination of flour, eggs, sugar, and butter, and then layered with generous amounts of Nutella. It's a decadent and indulgent treat that is perfect for chocolate lovers or for a special occasion. To make Torta alla Nutella, the cake is typically baked in a round or rectangular shape, then sliced horizontally into multiple layers. Each layer is then spread with a thick layer of Nutella, and the cake is assembled back together. The finished cake is then topped with more Nutella, whipped cream, or chopped hazelnuts, depending on preference. Torta alla Nutella is a crowd-pleaser and is perfect for birthdays, holidays, or any occasion that calls for a sweet treat. Its rich, chocolatey flavor and creamy texture make it a beloved dessert in Italy and around the world.

prep time

30 min

cook time

25-30 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Desserts

instructions

step 1

Preheat the oven:Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and prepare a round or rectangular cake pan by greasing it with butter or cooking spray.

step 2

Mix the batter: In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and baking powder. In another bowl, beat the eggs and melted butter together, then gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients until well combined.

step 3

Bake the cake:Pour the cake batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.

step 4

Prepare the filling: While the cake is baking, prepare the Nutella filling by scooping out a generous amount of Nutella into a mixing bowl. Use a hand mixer to beat the Nutella until it becomes smooth and creamy.

step 5

Assemble the cake: Once the cake is cooled, slice it horizontally into multiple layers. Spread a thick layer of Nutella on top of each layer, then stack the layers back together to form the cake.

step 6

Decorate and serve: Spread more Nutella on top of the cake and decorate with whipped cream, chopped hazelnuts, or fresh berries. Slice the cake and serve at room temperature, or chill in the refrigerator for a firmer texture. Enjoy!

ingredients

200g all-purpose flour

150g granulated sugar

2 teaspoons baking powder

2 large eggs

120ml olive oil

120g unsalted butter, melted

200g Nutella (or any chocolate-hazelnut spread)

Optional: whipped cream, chopped hazelnuts, or fresh berries (for decoration)

Note that you can customize the ingredients based on your preferences or dietary restrictions.

tools

Mixing bowls

Whisk or electric mixer

Measuring cups and spoons

Cake pan (round or rectangular)

Butter or cooking spray (for greasing the pan)

Spatula | Knife or cake slicer (for slicing the cake layers)

Hand mixer (for beating the Nutella)

Grater: for grating the Parmesan cheese (optional)

Note that some of these tools may be substituted with other kitchen tools you already have at home.
