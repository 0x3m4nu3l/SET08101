How to make Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish made with layered sheets of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. The meat sauce is usually made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and Italian seasonings. The cheese layer is typically a mixture of ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese. The dish is then baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the pasta is cooked through. It's a hearty and comforting dish that is perfect for feeding a crowd or for a cozy family dinner.

prep time

1/2 hours

cook time

40 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Main Pasta Dishes

instructions

step 1

Prepare the pasta: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the lasagna sheets according to the package directions. Drain and set aside.

step 2

Make the meat sauce: In a large skillet over medium heat, cook 1 pound (450 g) of ground beef until browned. Add 1 chopped onion, 3 minced garlic cloves, and 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning. Stir and cook for a few more minutes. Add 2 cans (28 oz/800 g) of crushed tomatoes, 1 tablespoon of tomato paste, and 1 teaspoon of sugar. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Let the sauce simmer for about 15 minutes.

step 3

Prepare the cheese layer: In a bowl, mix 2 cups (500 g) of ricotta cheese, 1 cup (100 g) of grated Parmesan cheese, 1 egg, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt.

step 4

Assemble the lasagna: Spread a layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch (23x33 cm) baking dish. Place a layer of lasagna noodles on top. Add a layer of the cheese mixture, and then a layer of mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until you run out of ingredients. The top layer should be mozzarella cheese.

step 5

Bake the lasagna: Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

step 6

Serve and enjoy: Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley or basil, if desired. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!

ingredients

1 pound (450 grams) of ground beef or Italian sausage

12 lasagna sheets pasta

1 large onion, chopped | 3 garlic cloves, minced

1 can (28 ounces/800 grams) of crushed tomatoes | 2 cans (6 ounces/170 grams each) of tomato paste

2 teaspoons of dried basil leaves | 2 teaspoons of dried oregano leaves | 1/4 cup (60 ml) of chopped fresh parsley

2 teaspoons of salt | 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper

4 cups (about 1kg) of shredded mozzarella cheese | 1 1/2 cups (about 400 gr) of ricotta cheese | 3/4 cup (about 200 gr) of grated Parmesan cheese | 1/4 cup (about 60 ml) of milk

Note that you can customize the ingredients based on your preferences or dietary restrictions.

tools

Large pot for cooking lasagna noodles

Colander to drain pasta

Large skillet or Dutch oven for cooking the meat sauce

Baking dish (9x13 inch or similar size) for layering the lasagna

Mixing bowl for combining the ricotta cheese mixture

Whisk for beating the egg in the ricotta cheese mixture

Measuring cups and spoons for measuring ingredients

Aluminum foil for covering the lasagna while baking

Oven mitts for handling the hot baking dish

Serving utensil for serving the lasagna

Note that some of these tools may be substituted with other kitchen tools you already have at home.
