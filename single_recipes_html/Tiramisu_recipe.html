How to make Tiramisu'

Spaghetti alle vongole is a classic Italian pasta dish made with spaghetti and clams. The pasta is typically cooked al dente, then tossed with a mixture of clams, garlic, white wine, and parsley. The dish is usually served with a sprinkle of red pepper flakes and a drizzle of olive oil. It's a light and flavorful dish that is perfect for a warm weather dinner or for a seafood lover's feast.

prep time

1 h

cook time

0 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Desserts

instructions

step 1

Prepare the espresso: Brew some strong espresso and set it aside to cool.

step 2

Make the mascarpone filling: In a large mixing bowl, beat together mascarpone cheese, egg yolks, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

step 3

Whip the egg whites: In a separate mixing bowl, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form.

step 4

Combine mascarpone and egg whites: Gently fold the whipped egg whites into the mascarpone mixture until well combined.

step 5

Assemble the tiramisu: Dip ladyfingers in the cooled espresso and place them in a single layer in a large serving dish. Spread a layer of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and mascarpone mixture, ending with a layer of the mascarpone mixture on top.

step 6

Chill the tiramisu: Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2-3 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld together.

step 7

Serve and enjoy: Just before serving, dust the top with cocoa powder and garnish with chocolate shavings, if desired. Cut into portions and serve chilled.

ingredients

6 egg yolks

3/4 cup white granulated sugar (150g)

2 cups mascarpone cheese (500g)

1 and 1/2 cups strong espresso, cooled (360ml)

24-30 ladyfingers

Unsweetened cocoa powder, for dusting

Note that you can customize the ingredients based on your preferences or dietary restrictions.

tools

Mixing bowls (2)

Electric mixer or whisk

Rubber spatula

Wooden spoon or tongs: for stirring the clams and sauce

9x13 inch (23x33cm) dish

Measuring cups and spoons

Lemon squeezer: for juicing the lemon (optional)

Espresso machine or coffee maker

Note that some of these tools may be substituted with other kitchen tools you already have at home.
