How to make Aglio & Olio Pasta

Aglio e olio is another classic Italian pasta dish that is quick and easy to make, yet still bursting with flavor. The dish consists of spaghetti tossed with garlic and olive oil, with the addition of red pepper flakes for a touch of heat and parsley for freshness. Aglio e olio is a perfect example of how just a few simple ingredients can come together to create a truly satisfying meal. It's a great option for a quick and easy weeknight dinner, but it's also versatile enough to be served as a side dish or as part of a larger Italian feast. With its bold flavors and simple preparation, aglio e olio is sure to become a go-to pasta dish in your recipe arsenal.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

11 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Main Pasta Dishes

instructions

step 1

Cook the pasta: Boil a pot of salted water and cook spaghetti (or other pasta of your choice) until al dente. Reserve some pasta water before draining.

step 2

Cook the garlic and red pepper flakes: While the spaghetti is cooking, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the garlic is golden brown and fragrant.

step 3

Toss spaghetti with the garlic oil: add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the garlic oil. Toss to combine, adding reserved pasta water as needed to loosen the sauce and help it coat the spaghetti evenly. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

step 4

Serve with parsley and extra red pepper flakes: Plate the spaghetti and sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley and extra red pepper flakes, if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy!

ingredients

Spaghetti or pasta of your choice (around 400g)

extra-virgin olive oil (120ml)

6-8 cloves garlic, minced

1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Salt and black pepper, to taste

120ml reserved pasta water

Chopped fresh parsley, for serving (optional)

tools

Large pot for boiling pasta

Colander to drain pasta

Large skillet or pan for cooking garlic and oil

Wooden spoon or spatula for stirring

Garlic press or sharp knife for mincing garlic

Measuring cups and spoons for measuring oil and spices

Tongs for tossing pasta with sauce

Serving plate or bowl for plating pasta

Chopping board and knife for chopping parsley (if using)
