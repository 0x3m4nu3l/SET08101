How to make Spaghetti alle Vongole

Spaghetti alle vongole is a classic Italian pasta dish made with spaghetti and clams. The pasta is typically cooked al dente, then tossed with a mixture of clams, garlic, white wine, and parsley. The dish is usually served with a sprinkle of red pepper flakes and a drizzle of olive oil. It's a light and flavorful dish that is perfect for a warm weather dinner or for a seafood lover's feast.

prep time

30 min

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Desserts

instructions

step 1

Clean the clams: Rinse the clams under cold water and scrub them with a brush to remove any sand or debris. Discard any clams that are open and don't close when tapped.

step 2

Cook the spaghetti: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente, according to the package instructions. Drain the spaghetti and set it aside.

step 3

Sauté garlic and chili flakes: In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and chili flakes and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.

step 4

Add clams and white wine: Increase the heat to high and add the clams to the skillet. Pour in some white wine and cover the skillet with a lid. Cook until the clams open, about 5-7 minutes. Discard any clams that don't open.

step 5

Combine spaghetti and clams: Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the clams and toss to combine. Cook for another minute or two until the spaghetti is coated in the sauce and the flavors are well combined.

step 6

Serve and enjoy: Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley and a squeeze of lemon juice, if desired.

ingredients

600g spaghetti

2kg fresh clams

6 garlic cloves, minced

1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes

120ml olive oil

240ml dry white wine

1 bunch fresh parsley, chopped | Salt and pepper to taste

Note that you can customize the ingredients based on your preferences or dietary restrictions.

tools

Large pot for cooking spaghetti

Colander to drain pasta

Large skillet or sauté pan: for cooking the clams and sauce

Wooden spoon or tongs: for stirring the clams and sauce

Garlic press or mincer: for mincing the garlic

Chef's knife: for chopping the parsley and garlic

Lemon squeezer: for juicing the lemon (optional)

Grater: for grating the Parmesan cheese (optional)

Note that some of these tools may be substituted with other kitchen tools you already have at home.
