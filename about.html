The Story Behind Our Website

Welcome to "The Italian Way" a Food Recipes website, created by a cybersecurity student with a passion for slow-cooked Italian meals. The founder moved to Edinburgh in 2013 and brought in UK the love for the Mediterranean diet, which is reflected in the website's mission to promote healthy food in the UK.

As the creator of the website, the founder is always available to help with any questions related to business or personal recipe-related queries and can be contacted using the Contact Us button . Thank you for visiting the Italian Food Recipes website and we hope you enjoy discovering and trying out our recipes.
